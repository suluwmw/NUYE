Rustic Dance Mark Williams

Rustic Dance draws inspiration from folk-style music, featuring simple melodies and rhythms. The piece has a light, energetic character that suggests a joyful village dance. Its straightforward style and strong pulse make it engaging and fun to listen to.

Bits of Stardust Bruce W. Tippette

Bits of Stardust has a dark, mysterious sound, with a steady drum beat that gives the music a strong, serious feel. The piece builds tension as different parts come together, creating a sense of the unknown. It feels like a journey through something powerful and unexplained.

Canon Power Bob Lipton

Canon Power is built around the musical form of a canon, where melodies enter in succession and overlap with one another. Bob Lipton combines this traditional technique with a modern, energetic sound. The result is a lively and clever piece that highlights teamwork and musical independence within the ensemble. You'll hear some familiar folk melodies as well.

Terra Nova Richard Meyer

Terra Nova means “new land,” and the music is about exploring somewhere unknown. Strong rhythms and bold melodies give the piece a feeling of adventure and movement. Quieter sections help show the mystery and challenge of discovering something new. The music often grows in volume and energy, showing confidence as the journey continues.

The Cherry Tree Carol Traditional arr. Robert W. Smith

The Cherry Tree Carol is based on a traditional English carol that tells a gentle, symbolic story. The melody is calm and simple, while the harmonies add warmth and feeling. Robert W. Smith’s arrangement focuses on clear musical lines and expressive phrasing. Soft dynamics and smooth playing help create a peaceful mood.

Ukrainian Dance Modest Mussorgsky arr. Deborah Baker Monday

Ukrainian Dance is fast and energetic, inspired by traditional folk dancing. Strong rhythms and repeating melodies give the music a lively feel. The arrangement highlights excitement and movement throughout the piece. Sudden changes and accents make the dance feel playful and bold.
